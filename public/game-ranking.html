Game Ranking

My top 5 all-time most played games

League of Legends

Isometric MOBA with a huge roster of champions. I've spent a lot of time Summoner's Rift.

MapleStory

Colorful and casual MMO with great music and lots of classes to grind.

Overwatch

First-person competitive hero shooter that was awesome at release.

Legends of Runeterra

Super free-to-play collectible card game set in the League of Legends universe.

The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth

Unique top-down roguelike with immense replayability.
